What Inspires Me?

Breakfast

Berry Coconut Smoothie Bowls

The best way to start the day is with a big smoothie bowl topped with superfoods like chia seeds, flax seeds, and hemp seeds. These bowls can also be topped with fresh or dried fruits and walnuts for brain power.

Strawberry Fig Pancakes

Fluffy vegan buttermilk pancakes using applesauce instead of eggs, and plant-milk instead of dairy milk. This was topped with fresh organic strawberries, dried figs, and pure maple syrup. Simply delicious.

Vegan Bacon

This is an example of an eggplant bacon recipe I created. We can also make healthy versions of bacon using portabellas mushrooms, seitan, tempeh, or even carrots. They key is slicing really thin, making a great marinade, and cooking on low heat for a long time, similar to dehydrating for that perfect bacon texture.

Lunch

Cali Kush Dawg

One of my best friends owns a vegan hot dog company back in Brookyln. This one is my favorite called the Cali Kush Dawg with marinted kale, avocado, caesar dressing and coconut bacon. DELISH. The dawgs are soy-free and gluten-free as well. And really good.

Tomato Soup w/ Grilled Cheese

Tomato soup made from scratch because it tastes better and it's better for you to make your soups from scratch.

Patty & Coco Bread

Jamaican patty and coco bread. All homemade and veganized. BOMB AF

Dinner

Ethiopian Food

My favorite food in the whole wide world. This plate includes lentils, cabbage, beets, collards, kale, pumpkin, and string beans. Everything Eshi!

Soul Food

Vegan mac n cheese, mushroom gravy, cornbread stuffing, candied yams, and smoked collard greens.
